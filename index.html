Located in Sidewinder, Colorado, the Overlook Hotel is a scenic resort with plenty of attractions for the Spring and Summer seasons. Tour the award-winnning Overlook maze, relax and take in the scenary from the Colorado lounge, dine in the state-of-the-art Gold Room, or even ski the slopes of the Sidewinder Mountains. With luxury suites and countless amenities, the Overlook Hotel has guests wanting to stay forever and ever and ever.
